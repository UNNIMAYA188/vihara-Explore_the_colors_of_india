Best Time To Avail A Manali-Kasol Holiday Package

The best time to avail a Manali-Kasol tour package depends on the type of experience you seek. Summer (March to June) is ideal for sightseeing, adventure activities, and trekking, with pleasant temperatures ranging from 10°C to 25°C. This season is perfect for exploring Solang Valley, Rohtang Pass, and Parvati Valley, while also enjoying paragliding, river rafting, and camping. Monsoon (July to September) brings lush green landscapes and fewer crowds, making it a budget-friendly option, but heavy rainfall can lead to landslides, affecting travel plans. Winter (October to February) is the best time for snow lovers, as Manali and Solang Valley transform into a snowy paradise, offering skiing, snowboarding, and breathtaking views. Kasol also provides a serene winter trekking experience, with destinations like Tosh and Kheerganga covered in snow. For a well-balanced trip with clear skies and accessible routes, the ideal months are March to June for adventure and October to February for snow experiences, while monsoons should be avoided due to travel risks.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Srinagar

Check in at the hotel anf freshen up. Exploring Hadimba temple, Manu temple, Vashisht hot springs, and Tibetan monastry. Leisure time at mall road. Overnight stay at Manali

Day 2: Srinagar Sightseeing

Adventure activities at Solang valley, Visit Rohtang Pass (if open) for breathtaking snow landscape, Alternate plan: Explore Atal Tunnel & Sissu Village in Lahaul Valley, Return to Manali for an overnight stay

Day 3: Srinagar to Gulmarg

Experience thrilling river rafting in Kullu (optional). Visit Vaishno Devi Temple & Kullu Shawl Factory. Explore Manikaran Sahib Gurudwara (famous for hot springs & langar). Reach Kasol, check into a hotel/camp & explore riverside cafes. Enjoy a peaceful evening at the Parvati River.

Day 4: Gulmarg to Pahalgam

Morning trek to Chalal or Tosh Village for stunning mountain views. Relax by the Parvati River and explore Kasol's hippie culture. Departure in the evening via bus/taxi to Delhi/Chandigarh

Day 5: Pahalgam to Sonmarg

Trip ends with amazing memories!
