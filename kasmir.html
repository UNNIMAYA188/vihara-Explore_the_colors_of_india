Summer Season (April to August)

April to August are considered to be the best months to visit Kashmir. During these months the weather is pleasant and is perfect for sightseeing activities. The Kashmir valley is flooded with tourists during these months and one can enjoy outdoor activities easily. The most famous outdoor activities in Kashmir are river rafting, paragliding, tekking, mountain biking, hot air ballooning, etc.

Monsoon Season (September to November)

from September to November Kashmir experiences monsoon season. The valley is pretty much empty as the peak tourist season comes to an end during the monsoon. During these months one can get discounted tour packages to Kashmir. The place looks more clean and refreshed after the showers. Make sure to carry umbrellas and raincoats, if you are planning a visit in Monsoon season.

Winter Season (December to February

Kashmir experiences winter from December to February. Many people across the country visit Kashmir to see the fresh snowfall, places like Sonamarg, Gulmarg, and Srinagar receive heavy snowfall during the winter season.
