Package Overview

Kanatal is a beautiful village in India near Mussoorie. It is a magnificent place to travel on your holidays to get away from your hectic schedule and away from city life once in a while. This small village in Uttarakhand, India is the place to be in if you are looking for scenic views of snowcapped mountain ranges while sipping tea or having breakfast. Tourists travel to Kanatal to acquire peace of mind and dive deep into the beauty of Mother Nature at its best. We are offering you this Kanatal trip packages for five days where you experience all of this and more with your friends and family.

What’s all the fuss about the Kanatal tour packages?

The Kanatal tour packages are designed in a manner so that the tourists can enjoy all the things that are required of a vacation. The Kanatal holiday packages bring you the opportunity to explore the beautiful village of the state. Kanatal has some of those postcard destinations which would enchant you. The scenic views, the chilly weather, and the cuisine in here, all makes Kanatal one of the most visited places in India. Near Kanatal, there are some of the most famous places to visit, like Mussoorie, Rishikesh and many more. The place is perfect to spend a nice summer vacation with friends and family.

What all places would be covered in the Kanatal packages?

The Kanatal tour packages cover all the major tourist destinations which should be visited while here. There would be a visit to the famous Tehri dam, Dhanaulti, Kodia jungle, Chamba and many more. The Tehri dam is famous for its lush beauty and amazing beauty. The Kanatal packages even cover the famous temples in these areas. There would even be a short trip to Mussoorie and Rishikesh. The chilly weather and the beauty of the place in here, would not only mesmerize you but would even give you the peace of mind that is required while in a vacation. The Kodia jungle is one of the most beautiful places in Kanatal. It is the best place for having an adventurous trip.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Dehradun - Transfer to Kanatal

Arrival at Dehradun Airport. Transfer to Kanatal for check-in at the hotel. Enjoy the evening at leisure.

Day 2: Kanatal Sightseeing

Explore Kanatal and its surrounding attractions such as Tehri Dam, Surkanda Devi Temple, and scenic viewpoints.

Day 3: Adventure Activities in Kanatal

Enjoy adventure activities like camping, trekking, and bonfires. Experience the thrill of the hills with panoramic views of the Himalayas.

Day 4: Kanatal to Mussoorie

Drive to Mussoorie, visit Kempty Falls, Camel’s Back Road, and the famous Mall Road. Enjoy shopping and local cuisine.

Day 5: Departure from Dehradun

Check-out from the hotel and return to Dehradun for departure. Tour concludes with memorable experiences.
