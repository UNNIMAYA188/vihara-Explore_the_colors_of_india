Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Wayanad

Pick-up from Calicut Airport/Railway Station. Transfer to Wayanad, check-in at the hotel, and enjoy a relaxing evening.

Day 2: Wayanad Sightseeing

Visit Edakkal Caves, Pookode Lake, and Banasura Sagar Dam. Enjoy the natural beauty and adventure activities.

Day 3: Wildlife & Adventure

Explore Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary, trek to Chembra Peak, and enjoy an overnight stay at the resort.

Day 4: Departure

Check-out from the hotel and transfer back to Calicut for departure.
